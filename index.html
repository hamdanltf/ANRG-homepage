about

The Network and Multimedia expertise group of Telkom University has three research laboratories including Core Network Laboratory, Application and Security Laboratory, and Access Network research group. Access Network RG is a research lab that focuses its research on the fields of Cellular Networks, Ad-Hoc Networks, and the Internet of things. Access Network RG is located in room O302, Ararkula building (O Building), Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Telkom University.
